Our mission

Womenhance fights for gender equality. We work to develop and uphold standards and create an environment in which every woman and girl can exercise her human rights and live up to her full potential, along with protecting herself from dangers.

Who we are

Womenhance is compounded from Women and Enhance, founded and developed by 4 girls during the Shooting Stars Foundation Hackathon 2022.
